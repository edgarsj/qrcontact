Share Your Contact Information via Phone as a QR Image

How a QR vCard works:

A QR vCard is a Quick Response code that contains your contact information in a standardized format called vCard. When someone scans this QR code with their smartphone:

  1. The phone's camera recognizes the QR code
  2. It decodes the information stored in the QR code
  3. The phone identifies the content as a vCard
  4. It prompts the user to add the contact information to their address book

This allows for quick and easy sharing of contact details without the need for internet connectivity or manual input. Perfect for business cards, events, or any situation where you want to share your contact info efficiently!
