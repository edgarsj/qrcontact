How QR vCards Help You Quickly Share Your Contact Info

A QR vCard is a Quick Response code that stores your contact information in a standardized format.

Show the QR code image on your phone's screen and let others scan it with their camera:

  1. They point their phone camera at your screen
  2. Their phone recognizes it as a contact card
  3. They tap once to add you to their contacts

Works instantly, no internet required. Perfect for quick contact sharing at meetings, events, or anywhere you'd normally hand out a business card. Just pull out your phone and share - you can even just share your Instagram or other social media links.
